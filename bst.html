In the Unified Modeling Language (UML), a use case diagram is used to summarize the details of your system's users (also known as actors) and their interactions with the system.Suppose you are working in a software engineering team assigned the task of developing system for a bank. The system will be used for transactions handling, staff communication, management reporting and e-banking. Bank officers will be using the system for transactions handing and communication. The bank manager will be using the system to view management reports and communication. Bank customers will enjoy e- banking when the system is deployed.
